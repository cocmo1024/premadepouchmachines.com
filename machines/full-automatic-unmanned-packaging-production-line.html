Machine overview

A fully automated packaging production line connecting feeding, primary packing, inspection, cartoning, case forming, sealing and conveyor logic into one integrated layout. An unmanned line is justified when output, labor cost, hygiene or logistics consistency require automation beyond a single machine.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Food production, Daily chemical products, Powder lines, Granule lines, Liquid lines.

Target package: pouch, sachet, bottle, carton.

Options to confirm: Primary packaging machine, Checkweigher, Metal detector, Cartoner, Case erector, Case sealer.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
