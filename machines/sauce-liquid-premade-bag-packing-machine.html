Machine overview

A rotary premade pouch system for liquid, sauce and viscous products, combining finished pouch loading, opening, pump filling, sealing and output. Viscous products require clean filling, drip control and seal-area protection. Pump choice depends on viscosity, particulates and temperature.

Target products: Fruit juice, Tomato sauce, Seasoning sauce, Chili sauce, Hand sanitizer.

Target package: stand-up pouch, spout pouch, zipper pouch, flat pouch.

Options to confirm: Piston pump, Rotor pump, Hopper heating, Mixer tank, Nitrogen flushing, Liquid level control.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
