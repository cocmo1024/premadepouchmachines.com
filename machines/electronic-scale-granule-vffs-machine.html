Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 10-80 bags/min depending on bag size and weighing system with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Measuring cup, linear scale or multi-head combination scale accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Puffed food, Candy, Biscuits, Frozen food, Melon seeds.

Target package: pillow bag, gusset bag, block-bottom bag, roll-film pouch.

Options to confirm: Bucket elevator, Multi-head weigher, Linear scale, Output conveyor, Date coder, Checkweigher.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
