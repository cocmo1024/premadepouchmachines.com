Machine overview

A compact premade pouch packing machine for particles, solids and puffed items where the factory needs finished bag loading, filling and sealing with a smaller footprint. This compact format is useful for lower-volume SKUs, trial packs and factories with limited floor space.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Small food packs, Medicine packs, Cosmetic samples, Chemical samples, Solid pieces.

Target package: self-sealing pouch, stand-up pouch, zipper pouch, flat pouch.

Core options to confirm: Cup dosing, Scale dosing, Coding, Vacuum or gas flushing, Counting device, Output conveyor.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
