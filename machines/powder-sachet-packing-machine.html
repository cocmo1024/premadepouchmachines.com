Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 30-60 bags/min with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around sachet, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 1-100g or 3-100ml accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Seasoning powder, Medicine powder, Cosmetic powder, Food powder, Chemical powder.

Target package: sachet, stick pack, three-side seal bag, back seal bag.

Options to confirm: Auger filler, Dust control, Coding machine, Linked-pack device, Lower cutting knife.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
