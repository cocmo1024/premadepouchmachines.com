Machine overview

A double-line packing machine for projects that need two synchronized lanes of small sachet or stick-style production from roll film. Double-line systems are useful when SKU dose is small but the required output is above a single-lane machine.

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around small sachet, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Cup, auger, pump or scale by material accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the dual-lane sachet platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Granules, Powders, Small liquid doses, Seasoning sachets, Healthcare samples.

Target package: small sachet, stick pack, portion pouch.

Options to confirm: Easy-tear notch, Batch coding, Linked-pack cutting, Counting conveyor, Nitrogen flushing.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
