Machine overview

A rotary premade pouch machine configured for powder dosing, bag opening, filling, sealing and shaping in stand-up pouches, zipper pouches, flat bags and special finished bags. Powders need dosing control, dust management and seal protection. The rotary premade pouch format is best when shelf presentation matters more than roll-film economy.

01

Validate Up to 60 bags/min on smaller powder formats with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around stand-up pouch, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 1500g, 2500g and 5000g classes by configuration accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

Target products: Flour, Milk powder, Seasoning powder, Solid beverage powder, Chemical powder.

Target package: stand-up pouch, zipper pouch, flat pouch, three-side seal pouch.

Options to confirm: Auger filler, Screw elevator, Dust extraction, Nitrogen flushing, Date coder, Checkweigher.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
