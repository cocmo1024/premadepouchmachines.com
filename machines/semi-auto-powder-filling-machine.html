01

Validate 10-60 bags/min with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around bottle, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Vacuum feeder or Dust removal accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

Target package: bottle, jar, can, cup.

Options to confirm: Vacuum feeder, Dust removal, Auger filler, Conveyor, Weighing feedback.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
