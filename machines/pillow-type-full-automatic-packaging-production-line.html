Machine overview

A full automatic pillow packaging line that combines product feeding, horizontal wrapping, conveying and optional downstream inspection or cartoning. Use a full line when upstream production is continuous and manual placement becomes the limiting factor.

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate Project-specific by product pitch, infeed and film width with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow pack, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Automatic feeder or Metal detector accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 250L/250S pillow type automatic production line reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Food factories, Bakery lines, Daily-use products, Regular objects, Tray products.

Target package: pillow pack, flow wrap, retail pack.

Options to confirm: Automatic feeder, Metal detector, Checkweigher, Date printer, Cartoner, Case packer.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
