Machine overview

An automatic filling machine platform for bottles, jars, cans or pouches, configured with one or multiple filling heads according to viscosity and output target. Filling head count should be selected after viscosity, target volume and output per minute are known.

02

03

Confirm One-head, two-head or three-head configuration by output target accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Sauce, Oil, Liquid food, Daily chemical liquid, Creams.

Target package: bottle, jar, can, pouch.

Options to confirm: Piston pump, Rotor pump, Heated hopper, Mixer, Capping machine, Labeling machine.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
