Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate Project-specific by bag width, length and dosing method with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around small sachet, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Cup, auger or scale by product accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 320/898 high-speed automatic platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Granules, Powders, Seasoning, Food additives, Small chemical packs.

Target package: small sachet, three-side seal bag, back seal bag.

Options to confirm: Batch printer, Easy-tear cutter, Round-corner cutter, Linked-bag output, Output conveyor.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
