Machine overview

A PE film filling machine for liquid or paste products using tube film or roll-film packaging, suited to economical liquid packs and simple refill formats. PE film packing is useful when the buyer prioritizes film economy and basic liquid containment over premium pouch appearance.

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around tube film pouch, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Pump filling by viscosity accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 260T PE tube film platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.

Buyer checklist before quotation

Target products: Liquid refill packs, Detergent, Daily chemical liquid, Sauce, Oil.

Target package: tube film pouch, liquid refill pouch, single film pouch.

Options to confirm: Piston pump, Rotor pump, Heated hopper, Date coder, Discharge conveyor.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Product name, photos, density, particle size, viscosity or piece dimensions.
  • Target fill weight or volume, accuracy requirement and output target.
  • Package dimensions, packaging material, pouch sample, container or carton size.
  • Required add-ons such as coding, nitrogen, vacuum, dust removal, capping, labeling, cartoning or case sealing.
  • Factory voltage, compressed air, local safety expectations, line layout and available footprint.
