Visão geral da máquina

A fully automated packaging production line connecting feeding, primary packing, inspection, cartoning, case forming, sealing and conveyor logic into one integrated layout. An unmanned line is justified when output, labor cost, hygiene or logistics consistency require automation beyond a single machine.
