Visão geral da máquina

Máquina cartoning automática: guia industrial para validar dosagem, formato, material, selagem e saída com o produto real. A seleção deve validar fluidez, poeira, viscosidade, estabilidade do pack, qualidade da selagem, troca de formato e integração posterior.

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around custom package format, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Leaflet insertion or Date coding accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 120 automatic cartoning platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
