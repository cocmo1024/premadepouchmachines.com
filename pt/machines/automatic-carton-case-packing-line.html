Visão geral da máquina

Linha automática de cartucho e caixas: guia industrial para validar dosagem, formato, material, selagem e saída com o produto real. A seleção deve validar fluidez, poeira, viscosidade, estabilidade do pack, qualidade da selagem, troca de formato e integração posterior.

Referência técnica

Parâmetro Valor de referência
Typical modules Automatic box opening, automatic sealing, automatic packaging machine and conveying
Sealing method Tape or hot-melt adhesive
Goal Smooth, firm sealing across different box specifications

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around custom package format, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Case erector or Tape sealer accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the case opening, sealing and packing line reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
