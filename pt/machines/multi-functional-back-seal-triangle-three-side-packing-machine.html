Visão geral da máquina

Máquina multifuncional para solda traseira, triangular e três lados: guia industrial para validar dosagem, formato, material, selagem e saída com o produto real. A seleção deve validar fluidez, poeira, viscosidade, estabilidade do pack, qualidade da selagem, troca de formato e integração posterior.

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate Project-specific based on bag size and sealing style with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around back seal bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Pump, cup, auger or weighing by material accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 3 multi-functional specialty bag platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
