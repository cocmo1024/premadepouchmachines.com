Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around custom package format, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Tape sealing or Hot-melt glue accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 40F/560FX case opening and sealing platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
