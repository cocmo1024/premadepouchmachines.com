Visão geral da máquina

Máquina servo para pouches prontos: guia industrial para validar dosagem, formato, material, selagem e saída com o produto real. A seleção deve validar fluidez, poeira, viscosidade, estabilidade do pack, qualidade da selagem, troca de formato e integração posterior.

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate Up to 40-60 bags/min by configuration with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around flat premade bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Cup filler or Auger filler accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the servo premade pouch platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
