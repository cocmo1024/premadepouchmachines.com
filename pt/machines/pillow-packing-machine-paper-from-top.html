Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 20-230 bags/min by model and product with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow pack, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Date coder or Feeding conveyor accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the top-feed pillow packing platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
