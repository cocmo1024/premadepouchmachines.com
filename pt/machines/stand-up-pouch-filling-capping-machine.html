Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around bottle, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 2-station automatic quantitative filling accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the ZLD-2/6 stand-up pouch filling and capping platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
