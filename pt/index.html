Guia

Escolha por produto, formato e meta da linha.

Grânulos, pós, líquidos, chá, café, kits de peças e pacotes a vácuo exigem estratégias diferentes.

01

Start with product behavior

Free-flowing granules, dusty powders, viscous sauces, fragile tea, hardware kits and regular solid goods each need a different dosing and sealing strategy.

02

Then choose package economics

Finished pouches support premium retail appearance. Roll film VFFS and sachet machines usually reduce film cost. Flow wrap and shrink machines fit regular solids and bundles.

03

Confirm the automation boundary

A single machine can solve primary packing. A production line adds feeding, weighing, coding, checking, cartoning, case forming, sealing and conveyor logic.

04

Build the RFQ around evidence

Useful quotations need product photos, fill weight, pouch or film samples, target output, voltage, compressed air, footprint and downstream packing requirements.
