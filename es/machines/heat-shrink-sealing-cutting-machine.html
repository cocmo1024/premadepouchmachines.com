Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around custom package format, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Infeed conveyor or Shrink film selection accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 100 heat shrink and sealing-cutting platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
