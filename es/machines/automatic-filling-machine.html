Resumen de la máquina

An automatic filling machine platform for bottles, jars, cans or pouches, configured with one or multiple filling heads according to viscosity and output target. Filling head count should be selected after viscosity, target volume and output per minute are known.

Target package: bottle, jar, can, pouch.

Core options to confirm: Piston pump, Rotor pump, Heated hopper, Mixer, Capping machine, Labeling machine.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Nombre del producto, fotos, densidad, tamaño de partícula, viscosidad o dimensiones.
