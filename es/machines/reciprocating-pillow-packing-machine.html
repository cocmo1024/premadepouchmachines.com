Resumen de la máquina

Máquina pillow pack reciprocante: guía industrial para validar la dosificación, el formato, el material, el sellado y la salida con el producto real. La selección debe validar fluidez, polvo, viscosidad, estabilidad del envase, calidad de sello, cambio de formato y conexión con equipos posteriores.

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow pack, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Automatic feeder or Date printer accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the reciprocating pillow packing platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
