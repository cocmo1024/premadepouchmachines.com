Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate Project-specific by product size and film type with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around shrink pack, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Infeed conveyor or Collation unit accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the full-servo heat shrink platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
