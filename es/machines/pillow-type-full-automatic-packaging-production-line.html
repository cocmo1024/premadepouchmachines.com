Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate Project-specific by product pitch, infeed and film width with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow pack, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Automatic feeder or Metal detector accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 250L/250S pillow type automatic production line reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
