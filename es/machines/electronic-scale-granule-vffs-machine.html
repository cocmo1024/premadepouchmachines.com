Resumen de la máquina

A roll-film vertical form fill seal machine paired with electronic weighing for puffed food, fruit, candy, biscuits, frozen food, melon seeds, roasted nuts and similar granules. Electronic Scale Granule VFFS Packing Machine should be selected around product flow, fill accuracy, package style, seal quality and the level of downstream automation required.
