Resumen de la máquina

Sistema industrial de Máquinas VFFS basado en , con selección de dosificación, formato, material, sellado y salida según el producto real. La selección debe validar fluidez, polvo, viscosidad, estabilidad del envase, calidad de sello, cambio de formato y conexión con equipos posteriores.

Parámetro Valor de referencia
Tamaño de bolsa L80-450mm and W80-350mm by frame size
Velocidad de referencia 10-80 bags/min depending on bag size and weighing system
Materiales OPP/PE, PET/VM, PET/PE, OPP/CPP and composite roll films
Dosificación Measuring cup, linear scale or multi-head combination scale

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 10-80 bags/min depending on bag size and weighing system with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Measuring cup, linear scale or multi-head combination scale accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 420/520/620/720K electronic scale VFFS platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
