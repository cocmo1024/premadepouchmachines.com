What the symptom can indicate

01

A high gas-flow reading does not prove that air is displaced from product voids and package corners.

02

Sampling time and probe technique can create inconsistent readings between operators.

03

Low initial oxygen can rise because of a small leak or inadequate film barrier.

Buyer analysis

Validate the measurement system first

Confirm analyzer calibration, sampling needle, sealant patch, sample location and time after sealing. Use the same method for every trial point.

Map purge geometry and timing

Observe nozzle depth, pouch opening, product bed and the interval between purge and final seal. Improve displacement before simply increasing gas flow.

Separate initial purge from package retention

Record oxygen immediately and at agreed later intervals. A later increase points toward seal or barrier performance rather than the purge event alone.

Observed pattern Evidence to check Controlled next action
