Compressed air Above 0.6MPa reference

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 10-30 bags/min with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around self-sealing pouch, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 20-1000g reference range accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the compact premade pouch platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.

Checklist avant devis

Produits cibles: Small food packs, Medicine packs, Cosmetic samples, Chemical samples, Solid pieces.

Emballage cible: self-sealing pouch, stand-up pouch, pouch zip, pouch plat.

Options à confirmer: Cup dosing, Scale dosing, codage, Vacuum or gas flushing, Counting device, convoyeur de sortie.

Envoyez photos produit, échantillons, cadence cible, tension, air comprimé et espace disponible avant le devis final.

  • Nom du produit, photos, densité, granulométrie, viscosité ou dimensions.
  • Poids ou volume de remplissage, précision et cadence cible.
  • Dimensions, matériau, échantillon de sachet, contenant ou carton.
  • Options: codage, azote, vide, dépoussiérage, bouchage, étiquetage ou encaissage.
  • Tension, air comprimé, attentes sécurité, implantation et espace disponible.
