Paramètre Valeur de référence

Film roll diameter Max 320mm reference

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 20-230 bags/min by product and frame with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow pack, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Extended infeed or Date coder accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the bottom-feed pillow packing platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
