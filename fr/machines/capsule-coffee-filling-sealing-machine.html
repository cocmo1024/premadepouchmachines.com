Paramètre Valeur de référence

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around bottle, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Vacuum feeder or Nitrogen system accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 1A capsule coffee filling and sealing platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
