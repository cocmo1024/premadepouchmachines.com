Paramètre Valeur de référence
Plage de dosage 1-100g or 3-100ml
Format sachet L30-170 x W30-130mm
Cadence de référence 30-60 bags/min
Temperature control Intelligent PID control with tight error range

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 30-60 bags/min with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around sachet, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 1-100g or 3-100ml accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 61F powder sachet platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
