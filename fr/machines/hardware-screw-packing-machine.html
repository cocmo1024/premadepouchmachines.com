Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate Finished small bags for assembly or retail kits with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around custom package format, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Counting channels or Vibration bowls accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 60T hardware screw platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
