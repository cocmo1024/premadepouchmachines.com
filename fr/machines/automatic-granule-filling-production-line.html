Target package: bottle, can, carton, shipping case.

Core options to confirm: Bottle sorter, Weighing filler, Top capping machine, Aluminum foil sealing, Printer, Labeler.

Send product photos, samples, target output, voltage, compressed air and available footprint before final quotation.

  • Nom du produit, photos, densité, granulométrie, viscosité ou dimensions.
