Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate High-speed operation by product and bag size with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around formed bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Automatic feeding or Date printer accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 35 T-type bag-making and bagging platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
