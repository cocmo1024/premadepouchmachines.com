Référence technique

Paramètre Valeur de référence
Line scope Primary packing, inspection, cartoning, case packing and conveyors by project
Automation goal Reduce manual handling across the packaging room
Layout Customized U-shape, straight-line or plant-specific routing
Integration Can include coding, labeling, checkweighing and case sealing

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pouch, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Primary packaging machine or Checkweigher accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the full automatic unmanned packaging line reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.

Envoyez photos produit, échantillons, cadence cible, tension, air comprimé et espace disponible avant le devis final.

  • Nom du produit, photos, densité, granulométrie, viscosité ou dimensions.
  • Poids ou volume de remplissage, précision et cadence cible.
  • Dimensions, matériau, échantillon de sachet, contenant ou carton.
  • Options: codage, azote, vide, dépoussiérage, bouchage, étiquetage ou encaissage.
