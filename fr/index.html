Guide

Choisir par produit, format et objectif de ligne.

Granulés, poudres, liquides, thé, café, petites pièces et packs sous vide exigent des stratégies différentes.

01

Start with product behavior

Free-flowing granules, dusty powders, viscous sauces, fragile tea, hardware kits and regular solid goods each need a different dosing and sealing strategy.

02

Then choose package economics

Finished pouches support premium retail appearance. Roll film VFFS and sachet machines usually reduce film cost. Flow wrap and shrink machines fit regular solids and bundles.

03

Confirm the automation boundary

A single machine can solve primary packing. A production line adds feeding, weighing, coding, checking, cartoning, case forming, sealing and conveyor logic.

04

Build the RFQ around evidence

Useful quotations need product photos, fill weight, pouch or film samples, target output, voltage, compressed air, footprint and downstream packing requirements.

Applications

Des systèmes pour chaque sachet et comportement produit.

Food, snacks and grains

Premade pouch, VFFS, vacuum and filling systems for rice, beans, roasted nuts, frozen food, puffed food, biscuits, seeds and retail dry food packs.

Powder and seasoning

Auger, screw feeding, dust control and powder filling choices for flour, milk powder, protein powder, seasoning, solid beverages and chemical powders.

Tea, coffee and beverage packs

Triangle tea bags, thread-and-tag tea bags, drip coffee inner/outer envelopes and compact beverage packs with ultrasonic sealing and small-dose filling.

Liquid, sauce and paste

Pump-filled sachets, premade pouches, PE film packs, stand-up pouch capping systems and bottle filling lines for liquids, sauces, oils and daily chemical products.

Regular solids and secondary packs

Flow wrapping, shrink packing, transparent film overwrapping, cartoning, case forming and case sealing for retail multipacks and logistics-ready cartons.

Hardware and mixed small parts

Counting, multi-material filling, screw packing and kit packaging pages cover quantity control, mixed accessories and small industrial parts.
