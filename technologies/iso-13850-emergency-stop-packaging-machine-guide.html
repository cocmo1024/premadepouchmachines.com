Buyer analysis

Emergency stop supplements protective measures

It should not be used as a substitute for guarding, interlocks, safe design or routine production stopping functions.

Line boundaries must be clear

For integrated lines, define which conveyor and machine zones stop together, what energy remains, how reset is authorized and whether restart requires a separate command.

Market and compliance signals used

ISO 13850:2015

ISO states that ISO 13850 specifies functional requirements and design principles for emergency-stop functions independent of the type of energy used.
