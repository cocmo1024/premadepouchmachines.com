Обзор машины

A horizontal pillow packing machine with film fed from the top, used for regular objects such as biscuits, rice cakes, bread, mooncakes, medicines and daily necessities. Top film feed is suited to regular products that ride stably on the conveyor and need clean horizontal wrapping.
