Buyer analysis

Tablet fragility and dust affect counting

Send representative tablets to check chips, coating dust, static, feeder speed and count verification before choosing lanes.

Consumer-safety requirements belong in pack design

The machine supplier needs the final pouch or carton specification, closure requirements and label plan; those requirements should not be assumed from the tablet itself.
