Buyer analysis

Count verification must match the customer risk

A missing screw, double part or wrong component need different detection methods. Define reject logic and challenge tests before feeder count is finalized.

Geometry determines feeder feasibility

Length, head shape, oil, nesting and burrs affect bowl feeding and counting. Send the full part matrix, including visually similar variants.
