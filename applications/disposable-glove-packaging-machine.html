Buyer analysis

Presentation and count method must be defined

Loose, folded or stacked gloves require different feeding and verification. State sizes, materials, powder condition and count per pack.

Artwork control extends to the case

Size, handedness, lot, carton label and case label should be checked as one data chain when multiple SKUs share a line.
