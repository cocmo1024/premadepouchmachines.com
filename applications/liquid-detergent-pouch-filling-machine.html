Buyer analysis

Foam can become the cycle constraint

Detergent may need controlled fill profiles and settling time. Acceptance should define liquid weight, foam height, clean seal area and spill rate.

Test every chemistry family

Concentrated, fragranced and low-temperature formulas can change viscosity, compatibility and foaming. One water-like sample is not a useful line test.
