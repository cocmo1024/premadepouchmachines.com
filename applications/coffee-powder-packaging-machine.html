Buyer analysis

Ground coffee changes after grinding

Gas release, particle distribution and bulk density can shift with roast and time since grinding. State the normal packaging delay and test the actual grind range.

Choose format around serving and oxygen target

Drip bags, sachets and retail pouches require different dosing, filters, outer envelopes and gas control. Define the consumer format before comparing machines.
