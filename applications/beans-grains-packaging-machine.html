Buyer analysis

Density and piece size change the dosing route

Small uniform grains may suit a scale or cup, while mixed beans and seeds need weighing that tolerates piece-size variation. Send every commercial blend rather than one representative product.

Heavy packs need controlled discharge

Filled bags can stretch seals or fall poorly onto conveyors. Include bag support, drop height, checkweigher transition and case orientation in the line trial.
