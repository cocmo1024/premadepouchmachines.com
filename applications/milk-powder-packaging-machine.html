Buyer analysis

Powder state changes during transfer

Aeration, compaction and moisture pickup can shift bulk density between blending and filling. The feed system and auger must be tested as one process.

Shelf-life protection is a package system

Barrier film, residual oxygen, seal cleanliness and closure integrity work together. Nitrogen alone cannot compensate for a poor barrier or contaminated seal.
