Buyer analysis

Similar-looking parts increase mix-up risk

Diameter, cross-section, color and elasticity should be included in feeder tests and recipe controls, especially when many kits run on one line.

Kit completeness needs a defined verification method

Use feeder counts, weight confirmation, vision or a combined method according to part size and the cost of a missing seal.
