Buyer analysis

Rim cleanliness controls seal quality

Coffee fines on the cup flange can create weak seals. Cup handling, fill cut-off, rim cleaning and lid placement should be tested as one sequence.

Gas performance needs a measurement plan

Define nitrogen supply, purge position, residual-oxygen method and test timing. Record both oxygen result and gas use per accepted capsule.
