Buyer analysis

Length and stiffness set the infeed design

Paper and plastic straws can behave differently under friction and static. Send the shortest, longest and most flexible SKU for trials.

Individual wrap and bulk count are separate line steps

Define whether straws leave as single wrapped pieces, counted bundles or cartons so accumulation and verification are scoped correctly.
