Buyer analysis

Soft products need low-stress transfer

Product compression, spacing, surface moisture and crumbs affect infeed design and jaw timing. Tests should use fresh production samples, not rigid substitutes.

Shelf life belongs in the film discussion

Barrier, perforation, seal layer and pack atmosphere must be matched to the bakery product and distribution plan before speed is finalized.
