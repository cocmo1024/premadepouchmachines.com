Buyer analysis

Weight and surface oil affect handling

Part mass, diameter, oil coating and sharp edges determine feeder, drop height, film thickness and whether single-piece or weight confirmation is practical.

Part-number control is part of packaging quality

Define barcode, label, lot and reject requirements so a correct count cannot be packed under the wrong identity.
