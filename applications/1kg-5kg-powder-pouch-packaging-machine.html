Buyer analysis

Size the filler around density and refill rate

Provide loose and settled bulk density, target accuracy and hourly powder demand. Auger capacity, screw feeding, hopper volume and refill control must support the required good bags per minute.

Treat bag handling as part of dosing accuracy

Bag opening, bottom support and vibration affect how a heavy dose settles. The final pouch must remain stable enough for top sealing without powder entering the seal area.

Use reference ranges correctly

Published fill classes and pouch dimensions are starting points, not guarantees for every powder. A sample test with the final pouch should confirm dose, seal, bag shape, speed and discharge.
