Buyer analysis

Small doses magnify cut-off errors

A small amount of auger tailing can be a large percentage of a seasoning sachet. Verify individual-pack distribution, not only the batch average.

Dust and aroma require planned clean-down

Fine seasoning can migrate into seals and machine cavities. Define flavor change sequence, cleaning access and acceptable carryover before lane count is selected.
