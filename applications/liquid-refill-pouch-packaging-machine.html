What the project must solve

01

The lowest film cost may come with different shelf presentation, opening convenience and seal-control requirements.

02

Refill pouches can be difficult to convey immediately after filling because the liquid shifts and the pack has little structure.

03

Pump and nozzle selection must account for viscosity, temperature, particles and cleaning chemicals.

Buyer analysis

Choose the pouch architecture before the filler

Compare film supply, zipper or spout features, seal area, drop performance and retail display. The package decision determines whether roll-film forming, premade-pouch handling or filling and capping is the practical route.

Control the pack after sealing

Discharge height, cooling time, orientation, leak testing and case arrangement affect whether a soft refill pack reaches secondary packaging without damage.

Provide the complete viscosity and chemistry range

Send every formula that changes flow, foaming or compatibility, including concentrated and low-temperature products. One easy-running sample is not enough to define a multi-SKU line.
