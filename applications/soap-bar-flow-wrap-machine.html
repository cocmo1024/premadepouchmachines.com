Buyer analysis

Surface condition affects film movement

Fresh, soft or dusty soap bars can drag on guides and mark film. Final product samples should be tested at the temperature and cure condition used in production.

Secondary packing may become the bottleneck

If bars are cartoned or bundled after wrapping, define group pattern, carton dimensions and good packs per minute for the complete line.
