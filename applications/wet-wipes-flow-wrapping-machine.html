Buyer analysis

Product moisture affects seal design

The wipe, lotion loading, fold, pack thickness and film seal layer should be tested together because contamination or liquid migration can weaken end seals.

Infeed stability determines real output

Manual or automatic wipe delivery, spacing and orientation should be included in the scope; wrapper speed alone does not define good packs per minute.
