Buyer analysis

Desiccant exposure time matters

The material can begin absorbing moisture before the sachet closes. Define hopper residence, room conditions, barrier film and storage handling.

Small doses require verification

Granule flow, dust and cut-off affect individual sachet mass. Use time-ordered checks and seal inspection across a sustained run.
