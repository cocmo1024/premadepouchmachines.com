Buyer analysis

Stack quality starts upstream

Diameter, thickness, count tolerance and alignment should be controlled before the wrapper; inconsistent stacks cannot be corrected by seal settings.

Warm product changes film and seal behavior

State product temperature, moisture and cooling time so film friction, condensation and seal quality can be tested realistically.
