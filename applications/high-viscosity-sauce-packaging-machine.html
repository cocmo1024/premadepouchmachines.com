What the project must solve

01

Viscosity quoted at room temperature may not match the product at the actual filling temperature.

02

Particles, fibers and product stringing can change valve selection, nozzle size and cut-off behavior.

03

Nominal pump speed is irrelevant if dripping forces longer settling and sealing delays.

Buyer analysis

Specify viscosity as a condition, not a single number

Provide the measurement method and temperature, plus the largest particle or fiber. Samples should be tested after the same heating, mixing and holding process used in production.

Select the nozzle around cut-off behavior

Nozzle diameter, valve closure, suck-back, pouch opening and fill height affect tailing. Video from a realistic trial should show the period between dose completion and seal closure.

Include cleaning and product recovery

Define changeover frequency, allergen boundaries, acceptable retained product and whether heated or mixed hoppers are needed. These requirements can change pump access, hose routing and the practical line layout.
