Buyer analysis

Small doses amplify powder variation

Bulk density, aeration and dust can shift accuracy at gram-level fills. Real product trials should confirm auger tooling, hopper agitation and seal cleanliness.

Lane count should follow stable demand

Single-lane equipment can support varied SKUs, while multi-lane systems suit mature high-volume products with stable film and formula.
