Buyer analysis

Leaf integrity determines the feeder

Whole leaf, broken tea, herbs and mixed flowers differ in bulk volume and fragility. Test the blend most likely to bridge or separate.

Inner and outer packs are one system

Filter material, ultrasonic seal, thread or tag and outer envelope timing must be validated together. A good inner bag does not prove a complete retail pack.
