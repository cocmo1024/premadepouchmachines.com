What the project must solve

01

A material can meter accurately while accelerating wear on screws, tubes, valves and product-contact bends.

02

Wear can gradually change clearances and dosing repeatability before a visible component failure occurs.

03

A quotation without a defined wear-parts package hides lifecycle cost and expected maintenance work.

Buyer analysis

Send hardness and particle information with the sample

Name, bulk density and mesh size are not enough for a wear discussion. The supplier needs particle shape, hardness, contamination limits and expected annual throughput to review contact-part material and replaceable interfaces.

Make wear measurable during ownership

Identify inspection points, acceptable clearances, calibration checks and replacement triggers. The spare-parts list should separate routine seals and bearings from product-specific screws, tubes or valves.

Acceptance proves function, not service life

A factory test can demonstrate filling and sealing with the real material, but short trials cannot prove long-term wear. Contract documents should distinguish tested performance from maintenance assumptions.
