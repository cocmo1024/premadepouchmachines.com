Buyer analysis

Product geometry determines the feeding path

Short pasta can be weighed, while long noodles may require aligned feeding or flow wrapping. Send every target shape because breakage and bridging change real output.

Pack dimensions must allow product settling

Bag width, length, headspace and drop height should be tested together so rigid pieces do not puncture film or enter the sealing zone.
