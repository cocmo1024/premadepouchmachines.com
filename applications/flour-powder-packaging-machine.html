Buyer analysis

Flour is both a flow and dust project

Aerated density, bridging and airborne fines affect filling and seals. Plant dust hazards and extraction requirements must be reviewed separately from food-contact construction.

Use sustained trials for refill stability

Short trials may not reproduce density changes caused by screw conveying and hopper refill. Record time-ordered weights through multiple refill cycles.
