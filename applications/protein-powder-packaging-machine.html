Buyer analysis

Formula differences drive changeover

Protein source, flavor, fat and sweetener change flow, static and cleaning effort. A multi-SKU line should be tested with the hardest-flowing and highest-allergen formulas.

Size the line around commercial pack mix

Single-serve sachets, retail pouches and rigid tubs have different dosing and downstream needs. State annual volume by SKU rather than one average target speed.
