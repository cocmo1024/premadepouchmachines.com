Test chemical powder, veterinary powder and industrial powder under production-like conditions; flow, aeration, breakage, stickiness or viscosity can change feeder choice and usable output.

02

Validate opening, filling and sealing with sachet and pillow bag; record seal contamination, trapped air and presentation defects at the target speed.

Buyer analysis

Compatibility precedes machine selection

Provide safety data, corrosion concerns, dust hazards and cleaning restrictions. A food-style stainless frame does not establish chemical or hazardous-area suitability.

Containment must include feeding and discharge

Dust escape can occur before the filler and after sealing. Review transfer, extraction, reject handling and clean-down as a complete process.
