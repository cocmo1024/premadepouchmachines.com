Buyer analysis

Product straightness controls feed stability

Warp, length tolerance, splinters and surface friction should be checked before selecting automatic alignment and feeding.

Food-service kits may combine several items

If chopsticks, toothpicks, napkins or condiments share a pack, define each component and missing-item verification method.
