What the project must solve

01

Premade pouches improve shelf appearance but require finished pouch purchasing and pouch-opening reliability.

02

VFFS reduces film cost and supports continuous output but may not match premium pouch presentation.

03

The best choice depends on pack value, SKU count, changeover, output, zipper or spout needs and downstream packing.

Buyer analysis

Compare package economics at annual volume

Finished pouches and roll film have different material, waste, labor and changeover costs. Use SKU-level annual demand rather than one machine-price comparison.

Product behavior can override cost

Pouch opening, product fall, dust and seal contamination may make one architecture more stable. Sample trials should precede final payback claims.
