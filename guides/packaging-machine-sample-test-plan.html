Buyer analysis

Good samples make quotes more accurate

Send real product, density or viscosity data, final pouch or film samples, target fill weight and output target. Photos alone are not enough for difficult products.

Record what matters

During testing, record speed, rejects, seal quality, weight accuracy, film waste, cleaning issues and operator adjustments. These observations prevent vague acceptance.

What information makes the quote accurate?

Send real product samples, package samples, fill weight, target output, voltage, air supply and required add-ons before final quotation.

Can the same line run multiple SKUs?

Usually yes when the products share similar flow behavior, package dimensions and dosing method. Tooling, filling parts and sealing jaws define the changeover boundary.
