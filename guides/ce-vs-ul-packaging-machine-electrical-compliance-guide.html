Buyer analysis

CE and UL answer different project questions

A buyer should not treat a control-panel mark as proof that the complete machine meets every destination-market requirement, or treat CE documentation as a substitute for local North American electrical review.

Define the acceptance package

Ask for the applicable declarations, panel scope, wiring drawings, component list, risk documentation, manuals, labels and third-party services before order confirmation.
