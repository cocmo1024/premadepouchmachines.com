Buyer analysis

Match the dosing principle to material behavior

Cups meter volume, scales measure mass, augers control powder displacement and pumps move liquids. Accuracy claims require the actual product and operating condition.

Include refill and discharge in the comparison

A dosing unit can be accurate in isolation but unstable when starved, overfilled or delayed by the bagger. Test the integrated sequence.
