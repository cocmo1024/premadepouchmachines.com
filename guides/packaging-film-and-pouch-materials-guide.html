Buyer analysis

Machine compatibility is a material property set

Thickness alone does not describe stiffness, friction, curl, seal initiation or hot tack. Request supplier data and trial production rolls.

Approve a seal window, not one setting

Establish a range of temperature, dwell and pressure that produces acceptable seals despite normal material and room variation.
