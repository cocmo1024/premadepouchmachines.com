Buyer analysis

Suitability depends on intended use

The product type, temperature, contact time, cleaning chemistry and material function should be documented rather than relying only on a broad food-grade statement.

Build a product-contact material schedule

Ask the supplier to list metal grades, polymers, hoses, seals, coatings and lubricants that may contact product, together with available declarations or supporting records.

Sources and standards

FDA food-contact overview

FDA explains that food-contact substances can include packaging components, processing equipment and food-preparation surfaces, and that authorization depends on intended use and safety assessment.
