Buyer analysis

Describe rheology and particles

Provide viscosity at temperature, largest particle, foaming, stringing and compatibility. These inputs determine pump, valve, nozzle and cleaning access.

Choose container and closure together

Sachets, pouches, spouts and bottles change filling height, cut-off and downstream handling. Validate the complete commercial package.
