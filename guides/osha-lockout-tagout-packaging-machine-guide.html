Buyer analysis

List every hazardous energy source

Electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic, gravity, spring, vacuum, heat and stored mechanical energy may require isolation or dissipation before service.

Design choices affect plant procedures

Main disconnects, air dump valves, lockable isolation points, bleed-down visibility and access around maintenance zones should be reviewed before purchase.

Sources and standards

OSHA machine guarding and LOTO

OSHA states that machine guarding protects workers from moving-part hazards and that employers must establish energy-control procedures, training and periodic inspection for servicing work.
