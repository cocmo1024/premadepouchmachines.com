Buyer analysis

Convert cycles into accepted output

Include dosing time, rejects, refill, micro-stops, cleaning and changeover. Report good packs per operating hour rather than theoretical cycles.

Calculate by SKU mix

Bag size, product and options change speed. Weight annual demand by each SKU's validated rate instead of using the fastest product.
