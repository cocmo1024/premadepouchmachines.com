Buyer analysis

Define the oxygen result and test method

State analyzer, sample location, timing and acceptance range. A nitrogen-flow setting is not a package-performance specification.

Optimize displacement before gas flow

Purge nozzle, product voids, headspace and sealing sequence control air removal. Record gas use per accepted pack during trials.
