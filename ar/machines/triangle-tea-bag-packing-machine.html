Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around triangle tea bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 1-10g per bag on selected tea configurations accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 20DJ triangle tea bag platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
