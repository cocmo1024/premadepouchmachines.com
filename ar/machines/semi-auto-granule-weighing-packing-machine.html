Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 800-1500 bags/hour with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around bottle, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Bucket elevator or Foot switch accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 5K semi-auto granule weighing platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
