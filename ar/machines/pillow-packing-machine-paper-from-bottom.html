نظرة عامة على الآلة

A bottom-film horizontal pillow packing machine for soft, long or loose products such as towels, paper towels, dried noodles, chicken rolls, sausages and soft sweets. Bottom film feed helps products that are soft, irregular or hard to push cleanly into a top-film wrapper.
