Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 10-80 bags/min depending on bag size and dosing with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around pillow bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Measuring cup, linear scale or multi-head combination scale accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 420/520/620/720K electronic scale VFFS platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
