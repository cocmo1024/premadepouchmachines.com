Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 20-55 bags/min with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around triangle tea bag, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm Measuring cup, electronic scale or auger accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 18-KFC/18-KFY drip coffee inner and outer platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
