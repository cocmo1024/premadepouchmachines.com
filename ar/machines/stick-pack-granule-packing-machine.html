المعيار القيمة المرجعية
نطاق القياس 3-50g or 1.5-50ml
Seal form Three-side sealing or back-side sealing
حجم الكيس L40-110 x W30-80mm
سرعة مرجعية 40-80 bags/min

Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 40-80 bags/min with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around sachet, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 3-50g or 1.5-50ml accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 10/40/150 stick and small-granule platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
