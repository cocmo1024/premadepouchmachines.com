Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate target output with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around sachet, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 1-50g or 2.6-60ml accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 62 ultrasonic non-woven platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
