Maschinenübersicht

A powder sachet machine for food, pharmaceutical, chemical and cosmetics powders, using automatic bag making, measuring, sealing, cutting and counting. Powder Sachet Packing Machine should be selected around product flow, fill accuracy, package style, seal quality and the level of downstream automation required.
