Maschinenübersicht

A combination metering packaging machine for products with different material forms, such as fruit cereal, dehydrated vegetables, whole grains, seasonings and blended tea products. Multi-Material Packing Machine should be selected around product flow, fill accuracy, package style, seal quality and the level of downstream automation required.
