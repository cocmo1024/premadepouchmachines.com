Acceptance checks before order

01

Validate 3840-4200, 9600-11000 or 11520-13000 cups/hour by station count with real product samples, final pack size and the chosen dosing method.

02

Run seal and leak checks around bottle, film or pouch material, filling temperature and product residue at the seal area.

03

Confirm 10-300ml, 10-50ml or 10-25ml by station layout accuracy, cleaning access, product contact material and changeover time before final quotation.

04

Use the 4/8/12 cup filling and sealing platform reference as a starting platform, then lock voltage, air supply, footprint, conveyor direction and downstream add-ons in the RFQ.
