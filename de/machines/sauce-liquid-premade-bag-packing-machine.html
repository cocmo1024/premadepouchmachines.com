Maschinenübersicht

A rotary premade pouch system for liquid, sauce and viscous products, combining finished pouch loading, opening, pump filling, sealing and output. Viscous products require clean filling, drip control and seal-area protection. Pump choice depends on viscosity, particulates and temperature.
