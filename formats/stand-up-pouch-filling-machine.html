Buyer analysis

Stand-up appearance depends on pouch and fill

Bottom-gusset opening, dose distribution and product settling determine whether the finished pouch stands correctly. Test the final pouch supplier and fill weight.

Match the opening to the filling device

Zippers, narrow mouths and pouch stiffness can restrict nozzle or funnel entry. Confirm usable opening width, not only external pouch dimensions.
