Buyer analysis

Narrow packs amplify registration and cut-off errors

Small width leaves little room for seal contamination, print drift or product tailing. Test the exact dose and artwork repeat.

Lane count must include downstream handling

High primary output requires counting, collating and cartoning capacity. Define finished stick count and reject verification with the machine.
