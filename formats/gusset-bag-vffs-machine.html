Buyer analysis

Gusset formation needs controlled web geometry

Film stiffness, former design and fold control affect symmetry and case packing. Validate appearance with the final printed film.

Filled shape changes downstream handling

Dense granules and light snacks load gussets differently. Test bag settling, checkweighing and case pattern at commercial fill weights.
