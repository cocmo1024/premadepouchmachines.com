Buyer analysis

Film width and product volume define the forming set

Fill weight alone does not define bag size. Provide product bulk density, headspace and finished dimensions so tube and jaw ranges can be checked.

Rate the machine on accepted bags

Dosing delay, product fall, seal contamination and inspection determine good output. Do not compare only empty-film cycle speed.
