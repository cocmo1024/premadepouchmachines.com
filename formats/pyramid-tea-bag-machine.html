Buyer analysis

Three-dimensional bags need leaf-volume testing

The same gram weight can occupy very different volume across tea blends. Test the bulkiest and most fragile recipe for dosing and bag shape.

Ultrasonic seal and mesh must be matched

Nylon, non-woven and corn-fiber materials respond differently. Validate seal strength, cut quality and brewing presentation with final material.
