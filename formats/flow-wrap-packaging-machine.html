Buyer analysis

Product pitch is the primary control input

The wrapper depends on repeatable spacing and orientation. Define whether feeding is manual, indexed or fully automatic before quoting speed.

Select jaw motion around product and film

Rotary and reciprocating end seals suit different dwell and product-clearance needs. Test the tallest and most heat-sensitive SKU.
