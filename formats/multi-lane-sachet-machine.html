Buyer analysis

Lane-to-lane consistency is the acceptance unit

Average output can hide one lane with poor weight or seals. Report accuracy, temperature and reject rate separately for every lane.

Tooling complexity affects changeover

Lane width, forming parts, cutters and filling distribution determine setup time. State the SKU matrix before choosing the maximum lane count.
